Our Services

Recycling RAM

We specialize in recycling Ram, which helps to reduce electonic waste and minimises enviromental and re use the materials to reduce the need for new raw materials.

Motherboard components

We deal with power issues such as No power which is the case where the motherboard doesnt power on at all and Intermittent power which involves random shutdowns and restarts.

Hard drives

We recycle hard drives in a very secure way by using software to safely erase all data , making it unrecovable or Disassembling the hard drive to seperate various components, such as metal casing, magnets and circuit boards .
